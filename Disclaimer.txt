-- Instructions --
Simply place the "DevDokus--Stores" in your resource
folder and add it to your server.cfg. Add the following line.

ensure DevDokus--Stores
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-- Requirements -- (Optional)
https://github.com/DevDokus/RedM-UsableItems
Not all items you add to the list do immediately work
in your inventory. Therefor you need to add them to the 
database like Vorp wants it. Furthermore, you need this 
plugin to make the items usable. The UsableItems does just
that, it makes the items usable. You can easily add your 
custom items on it. Each item can also be configured with 
an additional amount of hunger, thirst, stamina and health.
